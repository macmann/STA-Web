About Shin Thant Aung

Shin Thant Aung is a strategy consultant with over 10 years of experience supporting businesses across Southeast Asia. As a management and strategy consultant, he advises multinational corporations, regional conglomerates, and institutional clients on market expansion, investment strategy, and operational transformation.

His expertise spans healthcare, automotive, financial services, energy, and technology. He has led over 50 consulting engagements, delivering insights in market entry, commercial due diligence, and competitive intelligence.

He holds a degree in Applied Accounting from Oxford Brookes University and has a background in Mechatronics Engineering, combining financial, analytical, and technical capabilities.
